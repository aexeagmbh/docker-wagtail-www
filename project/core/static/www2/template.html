Why use AX

  • Publish faster

    The moment the data is available, it gets converted into text. No more waiting for editors or translators. This enables instant updates in your publishing plattform or online shop.

    Our engine will always give to you the most updated content that it can pull out of his brain. No need to update it, we will do that for you.

  • Constant Quality for any number of items

    Once successfully implemented, text generation delivers a constant, guaranteed quality level of content - in any language. Even if you scale to hundreds of thousands of products or articles.

  • Move content production into your toolchain

    Content can now be handled like every other process: integrated into your techstack and toolchain. You can now dispense with human interaction in your workflow by using our import/export tools or our API for content.

  • Enable Global Scale

    By providing instant content in any language (13 for now ;) based on language agnostic data, you can scale into new domains without worrying about translators.
